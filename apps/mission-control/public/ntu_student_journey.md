# Wei's NTU Journey

Wei Tan is a second-year CS student at NTU using Sentinel AI to stay on top of Data Structures and Algorithms (CS2040). With mid-terms and project deadlines approaching, Wei relies on the Sentinel overlay to capture moments of confusion during self-study—whether debugging a BST implementation or reviewing lecture slides. Each capture feeds into Mission Control, where Wei tracks readiness and closes knowledge gaps before exam week.

This scenario illustrates the seamless loop: **study in any app** → **trigger capture (Alt+S)** → **get Socratic guidance in the overlay** → **see readiness and gaps update in the Brain** → **act on insights without leaving the flow**.
